Questions for further reflection...

Here are some questions which we hope will help you to think further the readings and sermon today. Perhaps you also have questions of your own?

  1. Have I ever experienced a moment when God's love felt personal and real to me—when my own heart was “strangely warmed”? What happened, and how did it affect my faith?
  2. What does it mean for me to love others “as Jesus has loved me”? Are there specific relationships or situations where I am being invited to show that kind of love today?
  3. Where in my life—or in the life of my community—do I need to trust that God is “making all things new”? How might I cooperate with that work of renewal?
  4. How can I, in practical ways, help the Church be known by its love? What small acts of service, kindness, or justice might express resurrection hope in my daily life?
